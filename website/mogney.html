About

Navio is a QR code based solution for indoor localisation, tailor made for use in retail stores.

Track buyers in your store to gain shopping behaviour insights and use those for advertising in real time.

What to do?

Select the store you want to navigate through on the Navio app.

Enter the store, insert your smartphone in the slot provided in your shopping cart, and scan the QR code nearest to you.

Select your destination or enter your shopping list, and enjoy your shopping experience, while Navio takes care of the rest.

What to do

Enter the number of floors in the store, the floorspace of each and the number of shopping carts in use by the store. Upload floorplans for each floor. (in PNG format)

Attach the slots delivered by us to each of the shopping carts, by following the accompanying instructions.

Paste generated QR codes at specified locations in the store, using the Navio Store app.

Pricing

Using Navio is absolutely free for you. The store defrays all the costs.

Pricing
